About ThunderHill Resort

Elegant Accommodation: ThunderHill by Casablanca - A Premium Pool Villa in Wayanad offers a two-bedroom, two-bathroom villa with free WiFi and an outdoor swimming pool. Guests can relax in the hot tub or enjoy the private pool.

Comfortable Amenities: The property features air-conditioning, a fully equipped kitchen, washing machine, and TV. Free on-site private parking is available, along with a charging station for electric vehicles.

Convenient Location: Located 88 km from Calicut International Airport, the villa is near attractions such as Heritage Museum (10 km), Edakkal Caves (13 km), and Pookode Lake (26 km).
